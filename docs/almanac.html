It's a Full Moon Night

Author: coddfishh

The full moon has risen over Ravenswood Bluff. Hidden sins surface, hunters become the hunted, and even death is no longer the end. Trust is fleeting, identities shift beneath the moonlight, and every choice leaves a scar. Survive until dawn... bsince it's a Full Moon Night.

Townsfolk

Weeping Woman

"Every tear remembers a name the village wishes to forget."

Ability:
Each night, learn every player who died tonight. For each, learn whether they have an evil living neighbour.

Notes

  • Learn every death.
  • Each dead player is checked individually.
  • Drunk/poisoned information may be incorrect.

Lovebird

"To be a lover, you must first be a fighter."

Ability
Each night, choose a player. Tomorrow, you are mad that you agree with them. You should vote and nominate whenever they do, or you might be executed.

Cheating Examples

  • Fails to vote when target votes.
  • Nominates differently.
  • Publicly contradicts target.
  • Doesn't use Ghost Vote when target does.

Butcher

"I hate spare ribs... and rotten spouses."

Ability
Each night, choose a player. If they are the Lovebird, or if the Lovebird cheated today, you are safe from the Demon tonight.

Vulture

"Dead men tell no tales?... They do."

Ability
Immediately after dusk, you learn which character died by execution. You may choose to permanently gain their power. If they were evil or drunk, you are poisoned tonight and tomorrow.

Notes

  • Only one consumed ability at a time.
  • Consuming a new player replaces the previous ability.
  • Abilities are always used as Good.

Predator

"For every evil gazes into the abyss... but what if the abyss gazes back?"

Ability
Each night, choose 2 players. If one is the Demon, the Demon does not kill tonight. If both are evil, a Minion dies.

Minions

Eskes

"Truth... or dare?"

Ability
You know who the Demon is. Each night, choose a player: learn their character, though the Storyteller may instead show any character.

Vixen

"Every fox eventually chooses a side."

Ability
You know the Demon and one Minion. Each night, you may permanently become Good. If you do, you must lead the execution of the Demon tomorrow or die that night.

Notes

  • If disabled by the Preacher, the Vixen cannot betray.
  • Once Good, remains Good permanently.

Barmaid

"There is no pain I know that a few jugs can't fix."

Ability
Each night, choose a player. They are drunk until dusk. If you choose an outsider, choose an additional player.

Demons

Lynx

"Maybe... I was never here."

Ability
Each night*, choose 0, 1 or 2 players. Chosen players die. If you choose 0, you become Invisible tomorrow, registering as a Townsfolk until dusk. If executed while Invisible, you appear as a Townsfolk of your choice, the game continues, and a living Minion (except Vixen) becomes the Lynx. If no eligible Minion remains, Good wins.

Notes

  • Registration only changes alignment checks.
  • The Lynx never actually becomes Good.

SinO'dess

"Every heart carries a sin. I merely choose to awaken them."

Ability
Each night, choose 2 players and one Sin: Poison them, Kill them, or temporarily swap their characters until dusk. Once per game, you may permanently swap their characters instead.

Notes

  • Temporary swaps revert at dusk.
  • Players die as their original character.
  • Only one permanent swap each game.
